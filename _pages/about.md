Hello, I am Junzhe Li. Welcome to my homepage. I am currently a senior undergraduate in [the School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science](https://eecs.pku.edu.cn/) of [Peking University](https://pku.edu.cn/), majoring in electronics information engineering. I am also pursuing a double major in economics at [the National School of Development](https://en.nsd.pku.edu.cn/) of [Peking University](https://pku.edu.cn/).

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer systems and security, with special focuses on building reliable and high-performance storage systems and using program analysis to locate vulnerabilities in deep learning systems. Click [here](/publications) to find more about my research.
